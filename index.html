Catharine Street Community Center


69 Catharine St, Poughkeepsie, NY

Offered for sale is the 69 Catherine St. property (also known as 150-152 Mansion St.), comprised of two buildings; a two-story house and a multilevel commercial building formerly used as a community center. The combined properties are on a 1.57 acre parcel. Both properties have municipal water and sewer. The property is zoned R-4.

69 Catherine St. — A two-story 11,679+ SF metal and masonry building, previously used as a recreation center. It includes classrooms for young children, computer labs, offices, a gym, restrooms, and a breakroom. The property has several exit doors and 400 amps of electrical power.

73 Catherine St. — A 2,461 SF Victorian house, previously used for offices. Built in 1920. Well maintained. Newer roof, gas boiler & updated electric. Front and rear staircases. Walk-out basement & walk-up attic for ample storage. 7 rooms. Includes kitchen.
